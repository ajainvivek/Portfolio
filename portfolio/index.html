Working Capital Analytics

The new and intuitive solution helps customers investigate and explore Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) and related KPIs in a way that was not possible before - Intuitively, interactively and in real time, on the go with an iPad. These KPIs are important indicators, allowing the customer to analyze the effectiveness to collect outstanding receivables. The application provides a groundbreaking and easy to use way to interactively build up flexible drill-down analysis paths to explore complex big data on a granular level.
